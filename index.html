Know the Rotaractors of IIT Jodhpur

The Rotaract club of IIT Jodhpur was started in the year 2018 with the motive of helping and inspiring others.The faculity advisor of the club is from our institute Dr.Sushmita Paul and the club trainer Dr.Sushma Niraj Sethia is from our parent-club ,ROTARY CLUB OF JODHPUR PADMANI. The immediate past president of the club is Sonal jaiswal. Currently the club is presided by Unnati Gupta aided by Rakesh Kumar Maurya as the vice president. Swarit Anand Singh being the secretary aided by Akanksha Mishra as the joint secretary helps the club to run smoothly and effectively. The club treasury is handled by Sweta chauhan, With Aman Mohtta being the project development director.
The membership director of the club is Shashank Saraswat, responsible for welcoming and nurturing the new talent into the club. Harshita Kasera along with his team : Neeraj Anand, Sanskriti Hedaoo, Nikita Singh, Sanjukta Bhattacharya,Sanskrati Upadhyay and Asha Rani maintain the virtual face of the club. Bandaru Santhu Rutvik being the media director, also has an active role in maintaining the virtual face of the same.
Akshita Varshney is the community services director. Harshita Kasera being the Bulletin-Editor director. Hemant Shrivastva being the public-relations director.Bheemraj Meena as the International-Relations Director.Aastha Kapoor handles the professional-development sector of the club. They work together to plan, direct and coordinate the activities of the community outreach programmes of the club.

Along with the support of our enthusiastic volunteers, The Rotaract club of IIT Jodhpur aims to bring about positive changes in the surroundings.
